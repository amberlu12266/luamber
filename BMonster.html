B-Monster Mobile App





Overview

I realized that it's current app doesn't provide value for its users and its current website is cluttered and confusing. This B Monster mobile app includes all necessary membership features and perks so users can accomplish their goals more effortlessly.

Type

IOS Mobile App / Personal Project


Scope

UX Research, UI Design


Duration

3 Weeks



Starting Off

I started going to boxing cardio studio with my friends. The classes and facilities are great, but I noticed a bottleneck in it's user journey. When checking in for the class, customers are looking for the class reservation confirmation email that is in the midst of the countless emails received each day. I decided to look into its service process, information flow and here I am designing an app that not only solves the problem but also streamlines the whole process!


Image Source: B Monster Official Instagram



The problem

1. FINDING THE CONFIRMATION EMAIL WITH THE QR CODE USED FOR CHECKING IN

Users receive a confirmation email after they have successfully reserved a class. The email contains the class details and a QR code for checking in at the front desk. We receive multiple dozens of emails a day so it's stressful and inconvenient for the users to have to find a confirmation email that was sent 2 or 3 days ago or even longer.


Screenshot of my inbox.



Most of my emails are in English instead of Chinese and I don't get a lot of emails a day compared to most of my friends so the confirmation email is a bit easier to spot. Imagine needing to find this email in a midst of emails all in Chinese. Of course people would be stressed!


Why do users get stressed out by this? It's just like the slight stress you get when you're getting your credit card to pay for something but it's stuck in your wallet and everyone is waiting. It's a common thing people feel - being unprepared or not ready when it's your turn. Solving this problem would enhance the user experience.



2. WEBSITE & APP

B Monster has a website that every user has to access in order to reserve classes and perform other membership realted issues. They also have a mobile application but the main function for that is to connect to the heart rate monitor which they offer to track heart rate and calories burned.



I found that they have completely different functions available. The lack of connection between the 2 platforms affects the user experience and how connected users feel to the whole brand. Also, their website isn't very user-centric but users have to frequently visit the website to perform a lot of tasks such as purchase pass, reserve class, enroll in waitlists, etc.



User Research + Key Findings

I collected data through in-person interviews with B-Monster customers including my friends and people I met through classes. After the interviews I concluded a few key insights:

  •   Often forget how many classes are left on their pass.
  •   Often forget where and how to reserve classes.
  •   Wish there was an app to complete all necessary tasks.
  •   Have favorite instructors but often forget their names.


Pain Points

  • "Forgetfullness" due to the website's information structure and functions being all over the place. There should be a place for current members to accomplish all of their goals.
  • Many users try to reserve for the next class right after their current one is over which usually happens right after they leave the studio when they don't have access to their laptop. Going on to their website through their phones is very inconvenient.
  • Some users would like to take their favorite instructors classes but it's not easy to try and remember their names so users have a hard time trying to reserve classes taught by their favorite instructors.

So, how does it work?


Display of Membership Information


Having the profile page as the home page allows users to quickly get to the QR code that's needed for checking in, to easily see the number of classes left on their pass and to be reminded of their next reserved class.



Status of Class Reservation


Allow users to easily tell their membership and class reservation status as well as clear CTA button.



Buy Passes


Users can view the details of each pass and directly purchase it on the app.




Schedule Classes


Users can view the schedule for the following week, reserve their next classes and select a sandbag position directly on the app.



Heart Rate Monitor


Displays workout results overview and connecting of the heart rate monitor.




Performers


Display of all B Monster performers (instructors). Allow users to view profiles and favorite them for future purposes.
